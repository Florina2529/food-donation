About Our Platform

Reducing food waste and helping people in need through technology.

Our Mission

Our Smart Sustainable Food Donation Platform connects food donors, restaurants, hotels, supermarkets, NGOs, and beneficiaries to reduce food waste and fight hunger.

Objectives

  • Reduce food wastage
  • Connect donors and NGOs
  • Ensure timely food distribution
  • Provide real-time tracking
  • Optimize resources efficiently

Technologies Used

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Bootstrap 5
  • LocalStorage (data persistence)
  • GitHub / Docker / Jenkins
